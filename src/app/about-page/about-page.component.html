About Me

Hi! My name is Sophie and I am the owner of Sophie's Sweets LLC. I first started my business in January of 2021, but I have had a love for baking for as long as I can remember. My speciality is macarons, but I also love to make cakes and cupcakes! I've also made a few wedding cakes while running my business. My favorite macaron flavor is Sour Patch and my favorite cupcake flavor is Strawberry Crunch! I'm currently majoring in elementary education and am so excited to have a career as a teacher, and I hope to continue my baking business alongside that. I'm excited that you're here and hope you enjoy my desserts!
